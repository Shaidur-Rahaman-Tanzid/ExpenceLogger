### Weekly Budget
- Tracks from **Monday to Sunday**
- Resets every Monday at 00:00
- Calculates all expenses from current week

### Monthly Budget
- Tracks from **1st to last day of month**
- Resets on 1st of each month at 00:00
- Calculates all expenses from current month
